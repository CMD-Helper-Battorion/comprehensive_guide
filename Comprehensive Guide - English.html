Battery Calibration

You can try to restore some of the lost battery capacity by calibrating it, but this won't fully return it to its original state. However, it may help improve the accuracy of the charge level reading and increase performance efficiency. Here's how to do it:

    Manually Calibrate the Battery

    • Fully charge the battery: Charge the battery to 100% and leave the device plugged in for at least two more hours after reaching full charge.
    • Completely discharge the battery: Use the device until the battery level drops to 0% and the device turns off automatically. Do not plug it into the charger during this stage.
    • Leave the battery empty for some time: After the device shuts off, leave it turned off for 5-6 hours to ensure all remaining energy in the circuits is drained.
    • Recharge fully without turning on the device: Charge the battery back to 100%, preferably with the device turned off. Leave it plugged in for two additional hours after reaching 100%.
